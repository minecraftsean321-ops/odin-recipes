Sayur Asem Recipes

Description

So this is an Indonesian food that easy to make and suitable for boarder student to save their money. This food is known since 18th century when the dutch still colonized Indonesian. The recipes originally come from a simple, fiber-rich dish for the common people.

The combination of a refreshing tamarind-based broth and ingredients such as young jackfruit, sweet corn, *melinjo*, and long beans makes *sayur asem* a symbol of diversity and simplicity. Over time, this dish spread to various regions, giving rise to distinctive variations such as the Betawi and Sundanese versions.

Ingridients

  • 2 siung Bawang merah
  • 1 siung Bawang putih
  • 4 buah Cabe merah (kl mau lebih pedas bisa di tambah)
  • 1 buah Terasi di bakar

    • Isian

  • Jagung Rebus
  • Melinjo
  • Kacang Tanah
  • Kacang Panjang
  • Secukupnya asam jawa yg di larutkan di air
  • sesuai selera (me : 2 sdm) Bumbu racik sayur asem
  • Air Putih
    •

Steps

  1. Haluskan bawang merah, bawang putih, cabe merah, kemiri dan terasi yg sudah di bakar
  2. Tumis semua bahan sampai wangi dan masak.
  3. Tuang air (me : 500ml dulu, kl kurang bisa di tambah) dan masukkan semua sayur.
  4. Koreksi rasa dengan menambahkan kaldu, garam, gula dan air asam jawa.
  5. Masak sampai semua matang.
